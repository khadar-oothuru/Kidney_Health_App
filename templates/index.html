Health is Our priority

Kidney tumor detection is a crucial aspect of medical imaging where advanced technologies are employed to identify and analyze tumors in the kidneys. Early detection of kidney tumors plays a vital role in providing timely and effective medical intervention. This process often involves the use of medical imaging techniques such as CT scans, MRIs, and ultrasound to capture detailed images of the kidneys and identify any abnormalities or tumors.

Kidney Tumor Classification

  • Normal: A healthy kidney without any tumors, cysts, or stones.
  • Tumor: An abnormal growth of cells in the kidney, which may be benign or malignant.
  • Cyst: A fluid-filled sac in the kidney, which may or may not be harmful.
  • Stone: Solid particles that form in the kidney, causing pain and potential blockages.
